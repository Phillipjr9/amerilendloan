# OppLoans.com Design Analysis

**Date:** November 2, 2025  
**Source:** https://www.opploans.com  
**Purpose:** Comprehensive design analysis for AmeriLend redesign

## Color Scheme

**Primary Colors:**
- **Deep Blue:** `#0033A0` (primary brand color, used in navigation, headings, CTA buttons)
- **Bright Orange/Yellow:** `#FFA500` (secondary CTA color, "Apply Now" buttons)
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` (backgrounds, card backgrounds)
- **Light Gray:** `#F5F5F5` (section backgrounds)

**Accent Colors:**
- Green (success states, checkmarks)
- Various pastel colors for FAQ accordion borders (orange, purple, teal, pink)

## Typography

**Headings:**
- Large, bold sans-serif font
- Deep blue color for main headings
- Clear hierarchy (H1 > H2 > H3)

**Body Text:**
- Clean sans-serif font
- Dark gray for readability
- Generous line spacing

**Special Elements:**
- Numbered circles for process steps (white numbers on blue circles)
- Superscript numbers for footnotes

## Design Patterns

### Cards
- **White backgrounds** with subtle shadows
- **Rounded corners** (8-12px border radius)
- **Generous padding** (24-32px)
- **Hover effects** on interactive cards

### Icons
- **Material Design icons** used throughout
- **Circular backgrounds** for feature icons (blue circles with white icons)
- **Consistent size** and style

### Buttons
- **Primary CTA:** Bright orange/yellow with white text, rounded corners
- **Secondary CTA:** Blue outlined with blue text
- **Hover states:** Slight darkening or color shift

### Spacing
- **Generous whitespace** between sections
- **Consistent padding** within cards and sections
- **Vertical rhythm** maintained throughout

### Responsive Behavior
- **Mobile-first approach**
- **Hamburger menu** for mobile navigation
- **Stacked layouts** on smaller screens
- **Flexible grid** that adapts to screen size

## Key Visual Elements

1. **Numbered Process Steps:** Large white numbers in blue circles
2. **Icon-Based Benefits:** Circular blue icons protruding from white cards
3. **Trust Badges:** Logos from third-party review sites
4. **Phone Mockup:** iPhone showing loan application interface
5. **Professional Photography:** Diverse, happy people in lifestyle settings
6. **Colorful Accents:** Pastel borders on FAQ items and content cards

## Typography Hierarchy

1. **H1 (Hero Headline):** ~48-56px, bold, deep blue
2. **H2 (Section Titles):** ~36-42px, bold, deep blue or white
3. **H3 (Card Titles):** ~24-28px, bold, deep blue
4. **Body Text:** ~16-18px, regular, dark gray
5. **Small Text:** ~14px, regular, gray (disclaimers, footnotes)

## Interaction Patterns

1. **Sticky Header:** Remains visible on scroll
2. **Dropdown Menus:** Hover to reveal navigation submenus
3. **Accordion FAQs:** Click to expand/collapse
4. **Hover Effects:** Cards lift slightly, buttons darken
5. **Smooth Scrolling:** Animated transitions between sections

## Accessibility Features

1. **High Contrast:** Blue on white, white on blue
2. **Large Touch Targets:** Buttons and links are generous in size
3. **Clear Labels:** Descriptive text for all interactive elements
4. **Skip to Content:** Link at top for keyboard navigation
5. **Alt Text:** Images have descriptive alt attributes

## Mobile Optimization

1. **Hamburger Menu:** Collapsible navigation
2. **Stacked Sections:** Vertical layout on small screens
3. **Full-Width CTAs:** Easy to tap on mobile
4. **Readable Font Sizes:** No text smaller than 14px on mobile
5. **Touch-Friendly Spacing:** Adequate padding between interactive elements

## Key Takeaways for AmeriLend Redesign

1. **Use a bold blue and orange/gold color scheme** for brand consistency
2. **Implement numbered process steps** with large circular numbers
3. **Create icon-based benefit cards** with circular icons protruding from white cards
4. **Add trust badges** from Trustpilot, BBB, or similar services
5. **Use professional lifestyle photography** showing diverse, happy customers
6. **Implement accordion FAQs** with colorful left borders
7. **Create a sticky header** with clear navigation and prominent CTAs
8. **Use generous whitespace** and consistent padding throughout
9. **Implement a split hero** with image left, content right
10. **Add eligibility requirements** in a prominent white card
11. **Use full-width blue sections** for process and benefits
12. **Include educational content** with circular images and article links
13. **Maintain clear typography hierarchy** with bold headings
14. **Ensure mobile responsiveness** with hamburger menu and stacked layouts
15. **Add hover effects** to cards and buttons for interactivity
